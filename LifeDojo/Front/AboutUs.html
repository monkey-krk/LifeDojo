Chris Cutter
CO Founder & CEO

Chris began his career in the behavior sciences, and has spent the past ten years combining quantitative research, entertainment and design to build evidence-based health & life skills programs that work. Whether leading a multi-million dollar Federal Promise Neighborhood Initiative in New York City, or sitting in a room with a single student discussing the importance of exercise, Chris is on a mission to ensure every human being on earth has access to world-class, proven to work health & life skills education.
